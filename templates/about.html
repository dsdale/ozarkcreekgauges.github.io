About

This website was created over 20 years ago by a local paddler, Bill Herring. The creators of the current version of the site are a group of senior computer science students at the University of Arkansas. Our goal was to maintain the quality of information on the site while adding some new features. We hope to remain a reliable source of information for the boaters in this community with information about current water levels for most of the floatable streams in the Ozarks and Ouachita mountains.

Banner and key photos are courtesy of Gordon Kumpuris.

Contact Us

To submit a new river or correction to the website, use the form to the left.


Please try to be as specific as possible with your message. Adding put-ins and take-outs, directions, length of river, and reference gauges will help ensure that our team adds the most accurate information.


Any questions about the site? Shoot us an email at: webmaster@ozarkpages.com
